DPWS, Narapally

Delhi Public World Schools are a chain of schools under the aegis of the Delhi Public World Foundation. An initiative by the top alumni of Delhi Public School Society, the Foundation aims to bring education back to its ‘Guru-Shishya’ roots.

Our Vision - A Broader Mindset For A Brighter Tomorrow

Delhi Public World Schools are committed to provide state-of-the-art education, incorporating Indian values and World culture, aiming to create global citizens as torchbearers of a dynamic and harmonious world.

Our Mission - Learning More By Teaching Less

Delhi Public World Schools aim to instil in each child a sense of inquisitiveness and exploration, and an urge for experimentation, observation and synthesis. We endeavour to free the learning process from the confines of the four walls of classrooms and take it further to activity workshops, resource centres, laboratories and library, teaching less for learning more. Every child is encouraged to inculcate the values of citizenry; a commitment to good health, high values and sensitivity to the environment. We strive to guide and support each child in their journey from primary to senior school and prepare them for the journey beyond school.

DPWS Academics - Maximising The Potential Of Your Child

Delhi Public World Schools have the best infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities, including well stocked libraries, Mathematics, Science and Language labs, Rooms for workshops and Open spaces for sports. In terms of teaching, Delhi Public World Schools follow the guidance of the Education and Resource Council of the Delhi Public World Foundation, which comprises eminent names in the world of education. Delhi Public World Schools strive to achieve the best academic standards in the world and your child will have access to the finest environment and facilities for drawing out and maximising their best potential.

DPWS Sports - An Endeavour to Produce Winners

At DPWS, we believe that Sports plays an important role in the life of a child. The qualities of leadership, teamwork and sporting spirit are learned on the sports field. Therefore, every child at a DPWS is encouraged to take up a sport being given a vast choice including Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Badminton, Lawn Tennis and Table Tennis; Chess and much more. Our sporting facilities are for both indoor and outdoor sports and include 360-degree running track, swimming pool, cricket pitch, cricket nets, football pitch, basketball, badminton and volleyball courts. In addition to Physical Education teachers, the school also provides professional coaches. The sports teachers do the 'talent spotting' to encourage children to take up the sport that most suits their interests and skills.

DPWS Co-Curricular – Excellence in Every Note

The Delhi Public World Schools system develops confident, well-rounded students with capabilities and the spirit to reach for the stars.

  • The curriculum provides a rich variety of co-curricular activities, which we consider not extra but an essential part of a child's education.

  • Indian & Western Dance, Indian & Western Music, Yoga, Martial Arts, Drama, Debate, Elocution, Art, Abacus, Aero Modelling, Cotton Spinning and Weaving, Pottery, Handmade Paper Making, Carpentry, amongst a plethora of other choices.

  • From time to time, experts provide valuable inputs from their respective fields.

Educational Partnerships

  • ENTECRES STEM Lab– (Science Technology Engineering Maths) - Develops creativity, innovation, application of concepts & polishes team building & leadership qualities.

  • Tie-up with CampusWhiz for ERP System - CampusWhiz India‘s one of the leading ERP product for School Management Systems has fulfilled our requirements which span across Staff, Students, Examinations, Attendance, Fees, Payroll, Inventory, Transport, Timetable, Library etc. Moreover, it facilitates an excellent tracking system for parents to be informed of their ward’s entry and exit from school, thus, taking care of the students’ complete security and safety.

  • Tie-up with Words Worth Language Lab – Our English Language lab shall not only augment the vocabulary but also focus on the finer nuances of the language like grammar, pronunciation, intonation, modulation and semantics of speech. The process helps the students build confidence and proficiency in the language.
