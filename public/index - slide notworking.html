Establishing The Right Environment

We believe in building a modern culture using both traditional & next-generation tools. So your child’s natural talents can blossom.

Pragmatic Learnings

Our teaching culture follows Gandhiji's 'Nai Talim' principles. The students at DPWS will be taught practically as well as theoretically.

Smart Infrastructure

An extensive e-library will be accessible from outside and inside the school. Classrooms will come equipped with interactive panels & e-modules.

Talent Spotting

Sports coaches will pay specific attention to every child's skill set to encourage him/her to play the sport he/she is naturally inclined to play.

Smart Monitoring

Parents would be able to keep track of the academic and disciplinary growth of their children via mobile applications.

Smart Collaborations

DPWS Narapally has partnered with the best organisations to provide top-notch smart education to not just our students but our teachers & parents as well. We have collaborated with CampusWhiz for an advanced ERP system; Words Worth Language Labs for efficient English learnings; ENTECRES for a fully-equipped STEM lab.
