Curriculum

The Curriculum of Delhi Public World Foundation adheres to the National Curriculum Framework 2005 (NCF 2005) and the CBSE curriculum. The Foundation believes that the classroom experiences of children are to be organised in a manner that permits them to construct knowledge in consonance with the child's nature and context, thus providing equal learning opportunities for all children. Teaching aims at enhancing the child's innate desire and acumen to learn. Activity is at the heart of children's attempt to make sense of the world around them. Therefore, every resource is deployed in a child-centric manner to enable them to express themselves, handle objects, explore their natural and social milieu, and to grow up in good health.
