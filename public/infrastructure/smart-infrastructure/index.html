Smart Infrastructure

E-Library

In addition to our library, we also have an extensive e-library which can be accessed by students from within the school as well as from home. Students can use the e-library to prepare presentations of their work which also helps them to improve communication skills.

Smart Classrooms

To facilitate access to global learning in E-classrooms with Interactive Panels, Delhi Public World School has taken a step forward to create virtual classrooms with e-modules and a two-way approach for effective teaching and communication.

Smart Monitoring

DPWS Narapally will use CampusWhiz Education Resource Planning (ERP) tool to record data such as attendance & unit test scores. Parents will get access to this data through CampusWhiz mobile application. A secure messaging portal will also be provided for by ERP, thus enabling parents to have confidential conversations with teachers and safely discuss the growth of their child.

Online Fee Payment

Integration with major banking organisations will allow parents to pay fees online from the leisure of their homes & offices.

Smart Collaborations

DPWS Narapally has partnered with the best organisations to provide top-notch smart education to not just our students but our teachers & parents as well. We have collaborated with CampusWhiz for an advanced ERP system; Words Worth Language Lab for efficient English learning; ENTECRES for a fully-equipped STEM lab.
